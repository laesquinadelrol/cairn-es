# Cockatrice
5 HP, 8 STR, 12 DEX, beak (d6)  
- Small, hybrid creatures resembling chickens with reptilian features.  
- Critical damage: the victim loses d12 DEX, if reduced to 0 DEX, they are turned to pure stone.
